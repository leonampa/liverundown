# [Act 1]

**[SCENE: An abandoned subway station. Dim emergency lights flicker. The air is thick with dust and the distant, rhythmic hum of a tunnel fan.]**

**[CHARACTERS: ELARA, KAI, JAX, MIRA, SILAS, VANE]**

**[101] ELARA** I told you the signal wouldn’t hold this deep underground.

**KAI *(checking a tablet with a cracked screen)*** It held long enough to get us coordinates.

**JAX & MIRA *(crouching behind a concrete pillar)*** Keep your voices down, they can hear the echoes.

**SILAS** The echoes are the least of our problems, Jax.

**VANE *(pacing the platform edge)*** Does anyone hear that? It’s not the fan.

**ELARA** It’s just the wind in the shafts, Vane. Relax.

**KAI *(taping the screen)*** No, she’s right. That’s hydraulic pressure.

**JAX & MIRA *(sharpening a piece of rebar)*** How far out?

**SILAS** Too far for comfort. We need to move to the lower level.

**VANE *(grabbing a heavy flashlight)*** We’re trapped if we go down there.

**ELARA** We’re dead if we stay here.

**KAI & VANE *(scanning the dark tunnels)*** There's no exit on the map.

**JAX** The blueprints are fifty years old; look for ventilation ducts.

**MIRA** Those are too small for us to squeeze through.

**SILAS *(kicking a rusted trash can)*** Then we fight.

**ELARA & SILAS *(drawing sidearms)*** Let them come.

**KAI** Are you both insane? We don’t have the ammo for a standoff!

**VANE** I’m with them. Better to die standing than in a vent.

**JAX** You’re all choosing pride over survival.

**MIRA *(pointing toward the tunnel mouth)*** Look! A light.

**ELARA** That’s not a patrol light. It’s too steady.

**KAI *(shining the tablet at the wall)*** It’s a maintenance hatch.

**JAX & MIRA *(running toward the wall)*** Help us pry this loose!

**SILAS** Finally, a bit of luck.

**VANE** Move, move! I hear boots on the tracks!

**ELARA & KAI *(heaving against the metal door)*** On three!

**JAX** One.

**MIRA** Two.

**SILAS & VANE *(bracing against the frame)*** Three!

**ELARA** It’s stuck—wait, it’s turning.

**KAI** Clear! Get inside!

**JAX & MIRA *(diving into the darkness)*** Don't look back!

**SILAS** Vane, stop watching them and get through!

**VANE** I'm going, I'm going.

**ELARA & KAI *(crawling into the shaft)*** Pull the hatch shut.

**SILAS *(slamming the door)*** Gone.

**VANE** Did they see us?

**JAX & MIRA *(listening at the vent)*** Not yet.

**ELARA** We need to stay quiet for at least an hour.

**KAI** My tablet died.

**SILAS & VANE *(sighing in the dark)*** Great.

**JAX** How much water do we have left?

**MIRA** Three bottles.

**ELARA** That won’t get us to the surface.

**KAI** We’ll find more once we hit the utility pipes.

**SILAS** If the pipes aren't drained, that is.

**VANE *(shivering)*** It’s getting colder in here.

**JAX & MIRA *(wrapping blankets around their shoulders)*** Don't stop moving.

**ELARA** Stay focused, everyone.

**KAI** Did you hear that?

**SILAS** Stop imagining things.

**VANE & ELARA *(holding their breath)*** Shh!

**JAX** Someone is talking behind the wall.

**MIRA** That’s not a patrol.

**KAI** Those are voices from the surface.

**SILAS & VANE *(climbing toward a grate)*** We’re near the street level.

**ELARA** Look up. Is that sky?

**JAX** It’s a manhole cover.

**MIRA** We can make it out!

**KAI & ELARA *(scrambling up the ladder)*** After you.

**SILAS** Wait, listen!

**VANE** It’s just rain.

**JAX & MIRA *(crawling out into the alley)*** Fresh air.

**ELARA** We’re alive.

**KAI** For now.

**SILAS & VANE *(stepping into the downpour)*** We keep moving until dawn.

**JAX** Agreed.

**MIRA** Let's disappear.

**ELARA & KAI *(disappearing into the shadows)*** This city is ours tonight.

**SILAS & VANE *(following behind)*** Then let's make it count.

**JAX & MIRA *(watching the street)*** We’re clear.

**ELARA** Let's move.

**KAI** Finally.

**SILAS** Don't look back.

**VANE** Never.

**JAX & MIRA *(running)*** Faster!

**ELARA** Don't lose them!

**KAI** I’m right behind you.

**SILAS & VANE *(sprinting)*** Into the maze.

**JAX** We made it.

**MIRA** Barely.

**ELARA & KAI *(finding shelter)*** Safe at last.

**SILAS** Not yet.

**VANE** What now?

**JAX & MIRA *(looking at the horizon)*** We start over.

**ELARA** We start over.

**KAI** Together.

**SILAS & VANE *(nodding)*** Together.

**JAX & MIRA *(looking at the sunrise)*** Everything changes now.

**ELARA & KAI *(whispering)*** Everything.
